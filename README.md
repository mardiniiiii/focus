## 📋 What is focus?

focus is a simple app designed to help you stay focused and save time when reading long pages. It works entirely on your computer without needing internet access. The app quietly tracks when you lose focus on your screen and can read long web pages or documents out loud for you. This way, you can follow along without staring at text for hours.  

You do not need any technical skills to use this app. It is made for everyday users who want to be more productive and reduce eye strain.

---

## ⚙️ Features at a glance

- Works 100% locally on your Windows PC. No internet or cloud needed.
- Detects when you move away from your screen or switch windows.
- Reads aloud long texts on web pages or documents.
- Simple interface with easy controls.
- Saves your focus data privately on your device.
- Lightweight and runs quietly in the background.
